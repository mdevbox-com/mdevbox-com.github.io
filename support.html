Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data will be removed. We recommend keeping regular device backups through iCloud or iTunes.

How do I remove ads?

Some of our apps display ads to keep them free. We are working on premium options in future updates.

The app is not working properly

Please try the following:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  2. Restart the app
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the issue persists, contact us with a description of the problem
